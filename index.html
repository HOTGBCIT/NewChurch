Project Timeline

Plumbing and electrical inspections have successfully passed, marking an important milestone in the construction process. Preparation for the slab and footers is now underway as crews continue to move forward on-site.

Once the foundation is poured, it will require approximately 21–28 days to properly cure. During this time, visible progress will pause briefly to ensure the strength and integrity of the structure.

Following the curing period, block work will begin, and the steel structure is expected to arrive in mid-May, launching the next major phase of construction.

Project funding was fully approved, securing the resources needed to begin construction. All required building permits were officially approved, allowing the project to move forward into the next phase.

A groundbreaking ceremony and prayer service were held, marking the official beginning of this new journey.
